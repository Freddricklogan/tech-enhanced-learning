Course Syllabus

Course Description

This 12-week course explores how technology enhances learning and enables remote work opportunities across various industries. Students will develop both the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to thrive in distributed work environments, while learning to effectively leverage technology for learning and professional development.

Learning Objectives

  • Analyze how technology transforms learning and work environments
  • Apply digital tools effectively for remote collaboration and productivity
  • Develop strategies for successful remote work and virtual team management
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of technology-enhanced learning approaches
  • Create a technology integration plan for a specific learning or work context

Weekly Schedule

Week 1: Introduction to Technology Enhanced Learning & Remote Work

  • Evolution of technology in learning environments
  • Remote work trends and statistics
  • Benefits and challenges of technology-enhanced learning
  • Setting up your personal learning environment
