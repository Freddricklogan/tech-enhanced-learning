Course Syllabus

Course Description

This 12-week course explores how technology enhances learning and enables remote work opportunities across various industries. Students will develop both the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to thrive in distributed work environments, while learning to effectively leverage technology for learning and professional development.

Learning Objectives

  • Analyze how technology transforms learning and work environments
  • Apply digital tools effectively for remote collaboration and productivity
  • Develop strategies for successful remote work and virtual team management
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of technology-enhanced learning approaches
  • Create a technology integration plan for a specific learning or work context

Important Requirement

Each student must schedule at least three check-in sessions with the instructor during the course:

  • Initial Consultation (Weeks 1-2): Discuss course goals and project ideas
  • Mid-Course Review (Weeks 6-7): Review progress and address any challenges
  • Final Project Planning (Weeks 10-11): Prepare for successful project completion

These check-ins are mandatory for all major projects and will ensure you receive personalized guidance throughout the course.

Assessment & Grading

Component Weight Description
Technical Projects 30% Three hands-on projects demonstrating technical skills
Case Study Development 25% Analysis and creation of a technology implementation case study
Collaborative Assignments 20% Group work simulating remote team experiences
Learning Portfolio 15% Documentation of learning journey and application
Participation & Check-ins 10% Active engagement in discussions and required instructor consultations
